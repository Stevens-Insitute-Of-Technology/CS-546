During my summer 2019 vacation to Hong Kong and Macau. I had the opportunity to Bungy Jump from the world's highest commercial point. At the time, whoever jumped from Macau Tower was inducted into the Guinness Book of World Records.

I was standing at the top of the Macau Tower, prepared to leap 233 meters straight down. It's so terrifying up there that people near me wet their pants, yet it's a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity that everyone who can afford it should take advantage of. The whole operation takes less than two minutes, but those two minutes are enough to make you forget about everything.

If I had the chance to do it all over again, I would definitely do it.
